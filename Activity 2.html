Stranger Things

Set in the 1980s, *Stranger Things* follows the residents of Hawkins, Indiana, as they confront strange occurrences involving supernatural entities, secret experiments, and alternate dimensions. The show blends nostalgia, suspense, and science fiction with heartfelt storytelling.

Key Information

Created by: The Duffer Brothers

Genre: Science fiction, horror, drama

First released: July 15, 2016

Network: Netflix
